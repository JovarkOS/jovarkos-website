Welcome to JovarkOS,

A GNU/Linux distribution based on Arch Linux with the goal of striving towards a out of box experience the Linux desktop has never seen before.

Download ISO

Built for your needs.

Smart Defaults

We provide sane defaults out of the box. Our philosophy is to give you as much as you may need, without restricting customization after you install.

Immersive Desktop Experience

Speed and agility are the names of the game here. Delve into your work with Vivaldi or Firefox, whichever you choose, right out of the box!

Latest software

At its core, Arch Linux's Core, Extra, and Community fuels JovarkOS's software repository, plus we add some of our own via convenience scripts and allow you to use our custom repository filled with programs from the AUR.

Fast, tuned for the desktop

We make sure to include the latest linux-zen kernel for the ultimate desktop Linux experience.
